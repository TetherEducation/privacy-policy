Backoffice Assistant – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 Nov 2025

Backoffice Assistant is an internal Chrome extension built by Tether Education to help the CRM team manage integrations with educational services. The extension is designed for authenticated institutional users only. This document explains what data we access, how we use it, and the safeguards in place.

1. Data We Process

Category Examples Purpose
Authentication tokens OAuth access tokens, refresh tokens issued by Keycloak Maintain an authenticated session with Tether services
Educational service credentials JWT tokens, session cookies, institution identifiers (RBD codes) Automate integrations and provision tokens to the correct campus
Basic user profile Name, corporate email, campus assignments Display account status inside the extension UI
Technical metadata Browser version, extension version, timestamps, success/error logs Troubleshooting, rate limiting, and audit logs

The extension does not collect browsing history, unrelated form inputs, financial information, or student personal data.

2. How We Use The Data

  1. Automated integrations – captured tokens are sent to the Credential Manager and Integration Core microservices hosted by Tether Education.
  2. Credential management – tokens are encrypted locally (AES-256-GCM) and persisted only for the minimum time required to deliver them to the CRM.
  3. Operational analytics – aggregate events (success, failure, timestamps) are logged to monitor service reliability. No advertising or profiling is performed.

3. Data Storage & Retention

  • Tokens and credentials are kept in encrypted extension storage (chrome.storage.local) and cleared immediately after they are acknowledged by the backend.
  • Server-side services retain encrypted copies following Tether’s retention policy (maximum 90 days) for auditability.
  • No data leaves Tether-controlled infrastructure.

4. Sharing With Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties. Data is transmitted exclusively to:

  • https://api.v2.tether.education/* (Credential Manager & Integration Core)
  • https://auth.tether.education/* and https://auth.dev.tether.education/* (Keycloak Identity)
  • https://app.tether.education/* (Backoffice web application)

All traffic is transmitted over HTTPS with TLS 1.2+.

5. User Controls

  • Users must authenticate with their corporate Google Workspace account through Keycloak.
  • Tokens can be revoked at any time inside the Backoffice Assistant popup (Logout button).
  • Removing the extension from Chrome deletes all locally stored data.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to contacto@tether.education. Provide the extension version and the review identifier if the request comes from a Chrome Web Store reviewer.

This policy will be updated whenever we introduce new data flows, permissions, or integrations.
